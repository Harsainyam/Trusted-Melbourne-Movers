Common Questions About Interstate Moving

Can I pack my own belongings for an intrastate move? +
Reputable intrastate moving companies allow you to pack your own belongings for a relocation. Customers can choose between self-packing, partial packing, or letting the movers pack everything with full-service packing. However, when you want to protect fragile items and save time during a relocation, it's a good idea to choose a full packing service.
Can intrastate moves be completed on the same day? +
Most intrastate moves can be completed in a single day, since the distance is limited to within state lines. However, same-day completion may not be possible for larger moves, or those that span the full length of the state. Your moving coordinator will provide an accurate timeline during your consultation.
Can intrastate movers transport fragile items safely? +
Professional intrastate movers have the expertise and materials to safely transport fragile items. We use specialized packing materials, custom crating for valuable items, and careful handling techniques to ensure your delicate possessions arrive intact. Insurance coverage is also available for additional protection.
How much does an intrastate move in Illinois cost? +
The cost of an intrastate move depends on several factors including distance, volume of belongings, services required, and timing. On average, local moves within Illinois range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific moving needs.
Do I need to be present during the entire move? +
While it's recommended to be present at both the pickup and delivery locations to oversee the inventory and provide instructions, you don't necessarily need to be there for the entire process. You can designate a trusted representative to be present on your behalf if needed.
