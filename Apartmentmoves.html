Common Questions About Apartment Movers

How long does it take to complete a small apartment move? +
A small apartment move typically takes around 2–4 hours, depending on the amount of belongings and complexity of the move. An apartment relocation may require more time, based on factors like elevator availability, parking restrictions and so on.
Can apartment movers handle tight hallways and stairs? +
Experienced apartment movers can handle small doorways, narrow hallways and tricky staircases without causing damage to belongings or the building. Movers may use equipment like furniture dollies and moving straps to help navigate apartment buildings.
Can I pack belongings myself for an apartment move? +
Absolutely—customers can pack their own belongings for an apartment move or choose a partial packing service. However, when you want to protect fragile items and save time during a relocation, it's a good idea to choose a full packing service.
