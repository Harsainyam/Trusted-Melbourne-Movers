Common Questions About Senior Moves

How far in advance should I schedule a senior move? +
It's a good idea to schedule a senior move around 4–6 weeks in advance to ensure availability and detailed relocation planning. However, if you have limited notice to move, you can also call to check availability at any time.
Do senior movers unpack and place items at the new residence? +
Reputable senior moving companies unpack items and place them in designated locations around your new home or nursing facility. Your moving team can also provide light furniture disassembly and reassembly for your convenience.
How much does a senior move cost? +
Senior moves will vary in cost. It depends on the distance, size of the move, and any storage or specialty services you may require. We invite you to request a free estimate for a transparent, up-front price before committing to a move.
